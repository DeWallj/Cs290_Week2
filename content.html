How To Put A Stapler In Jello

  1. Gather Materials
    • Lots and lots of Jello
    • A bowl deep enough for stapler
    • A stapler
  2. Create Jello Mold With A Suspended Stapler
    • Fill your bowl up halfway and allow Jello to cool
    • Place stapler onto cooled Jello
    • Fill the remainder of the bowl with another round of Jello and allow to cool
    • Turn bowl upside down to remove mold
    • Place mold where friend or colleague would be most annoyed
